# Client Outreach Pack — metrics & permissions for the site revamp

The redesign is proof-led: every number on the site must be client-approved or
self-owned. One invented statistic destroys a proof-led brand. This is the ask
list — start these conversations now; everything else on the roadmap can ship
while answers come in.

## What to ask every client

1. **Permission** to name them and show their tour on beyex.com (most already
   appear — this makes it explicit and gets it in writing).
2. **Logo permission** for a "trusted by" strip.
3. **One outcome metric** they'd stand behind, e.g.:
   - change in direct bookings / enquiries / viewings since the tour went live
   - website dwell time on pages with the tour vs without
   - site visits or pre-visit questions avoided
4. **One quotable sentence** from a named person with job title.
5. **Capture facts** we can self-verify and publish as data plates:
   floor area scanned, number of scan points, capture date, turnaround.

## Email template

> Subject: Your 3D tour is front and centre on our new site — two quick asks
>
> Hi [name],
>
> We're relaunching beyex.com around real client work, and [client]'s tour is
> one of the captures we're proudest of.
>
> Two quick asks:
> 1. Are you happy for us to keep showing the tour with your name (and logo)?
> 2. Has the tour moved any numbers for you — bookings, enquiries, viewings,
>    time saved? Even a rough "enquiries are up about X% since it went live"
>    with your name on it is gold. We'll only publish what you sign off.
>
> Happy to return the favour — a fresh capture of any new space, on us.
>
> [sign-off]
